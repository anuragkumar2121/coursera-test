PROCESS TO MAKE TEA

So here are steps to make tea:
  1. Take water in a pan and boil it
    1. Ideally boil till 96 degree centigrade
  2. Add tea bag or tea leavees to it
  3. Wait for 5-10 minutes for strong flavours
    1. Add milk if you like but don't exceed 80:20 ratio of milk to water
  4. Serve it hot
